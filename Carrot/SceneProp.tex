The equations for the moving car are\\

$\Delta{t} = 1$\\
$x(t) = x(t-1) + \dot{x}(t-1) + \frac{1}{2} \ddot{x}(t-1)$\\
$\ddot{x} \sim N(0,1)$\\
$x(t) = x(t-1) + \dot{x}(t-1) + \frac{1}{2} N(0,1)$\\
$\dot{x}(t) = \dot{x}(t-1) + \ddot{x}(t-1) \Delta{t} = \dot{x}(t-1) + \ddot{x}(t-1)$\\
